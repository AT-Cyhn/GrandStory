GrandStory
Developer: Andy Tan
Date: November 12, 2013

A two-player "street fighter" game. Whoever puts the opposing player's health to zero wins.

Player1 Controls:
R - jump
D - move left
G - move right
F - drop down from platform/faster drop
A - attack button
hold A - charge energy bar
double tap D - dash left
double tap G - dash right

Player2 Controls:
Keypad 8 - jump
Keypad 4 - move left
Keypad 6 - move right
Keypad 5 - drop down from platform/faster drop
L - attack button
hold L - charge energy bar
double tap Keypad 4 - dash left
double tap Keypad 6 - dash right
